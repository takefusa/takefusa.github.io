# Atsuko Takefusa / 竹房 あつ子

## About Me
Atsuko Takefusa, Ph.D. is a professor at 
[the National Institute of Informatics (NII)](https://www.nii.ac.jp/) and
[the Graduate University for Advanced Studies (SOKENDAI)](https://www.soken.ac.jp/), in Tokyo, Japan.
She has also served as a team principal of 
[the High Performance Cloud Systems and Secure Software Research Team](https://www.r-ccs.riken.jp/en/research/labs/hpcsssrt/index.html), 
[the RIKEN Center for Computational Science](https://www.r-ccs.riken.jp/en/).
Her research interests are parallel distributed computing, HPC, Grid, Cloud, and IoT.
